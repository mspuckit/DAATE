The purpose for this policy is to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect that information, how it is used once collected, and what action you can take to control of and protect your personal information while using DAATE (referred to hereafter as "the Site").


PERSONAL INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

When you visit the Site, we do NOT collect information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and cookies that are installed on your device. Additionally, as you browse the Site, we do NOT collect information about the individual web pages, dashboards or dashboard filters you view or how you interact with the Site.



PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you visit the site and enter name and email address in the Contact form, and select Send Message, this information is sent to a Outlook mailbox outside of the Site. When we talk about "Personal Information" in this Privacy Policy, we are talking about the name and email address entered when submitting a form through the "Contact" section of the Site.


HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We may use the name and email address supplied via the Contact form to respond to your questions or comments.

DAATE will never send unsolicited updates or notifications regarding content, marketing communications or newsletters. If you feel you are receiving updates or emails from DAATE please contact us at ucbresearchequity@outlook.com.


SHARING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We do NOT share your Personal Information with third parties.

DAATE and the corresponding email account is currently hosted and administered within the United States. By using our service, you authorize DAATE to transfer and store your information in the United States, for the purposes described in this policy. The privacy protections and the rights of authorities to access your personal information in such countries may not be equivalent to those of your home country.

Finally, we may share your Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.


YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information below.


DATA RETENTION

When you submit through the contact form of the Site, we will maintain your feedback for our records, and the original email will remain outside of the Site in the mailbox of the account associated with the Site unless and until you ask us to delete this information.


MINORS

DAATE is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not seek to collect any information from or about those individuals. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information of or from your minor child, please contact us at ucbresearchquity@outlook.com.


UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.


CONTACT US

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at ucbresearchequity@outlook.com
