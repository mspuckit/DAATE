  • DAATE is intended to provide a deeper, more contextual perspective for defense counsel in the consideration of relevant factors and mitigating circumstances that may impact sentencing.
    • DAATE is not an assessment of individual judges and may not be indicative of an individual's entire judicial record.
    • DAATE is a contextual aggregation of sentencing information pulled from Florida Department of Corrections (2004-2016) to aid in a comprehensive assessment of potential sentencing outcomes as a reflection of prior sentencing behavior.
  • DAATE is not a risk assessment tool and does not replace or reduce the need for qualified, licensed legal counsel and broader situational analysis in any way.
    • DAATE should be used as a supplemental survey tool directed at better understanding past sentencing trends and predicting potential likelihood of future sentencing behavior for use in the overall judicial defense process.
